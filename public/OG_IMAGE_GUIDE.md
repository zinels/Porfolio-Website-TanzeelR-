# Open Graph Image Guide

## What is an Open Graph Image?

When you share your website on social media (LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, etc.), the Open Graph image is what appears in the preview card. This is the first thing people see when your link is shared!

## Current Setup

Your portfolio is configured to use `/og-image.jpg` as the Open Graph image.

## How to Add Your Professional Image

1. **Create or get a professional image:**
   - Size: **1200x630 pixels** (recommended)
   - Format: JPG or PNG
   - Content: Should include your name, title, and look professional

2. **Save it as:**
   - File name: `og-image.jpg`
   - Location: `public/og-image.jpg`

3. **What to include in the image:**
   - Your name: "Tanzeel Rahman"
   - Your title: "Software Engineer"
   - Professional design/background
   - Maybe a subtle logo or pattern
   - Keep it clean and readable
